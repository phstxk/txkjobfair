💡 Tips for Applying

  • Apply online before showing up in person — most employers prefer it.
  • If you're 14–15, Texas requires a Certificate of Age from the Texas Workforce Commission.
  • Arkansas teens 14–15 may need parental consent forms — check with each employer.
  • Bring a parent/guardian's contact info — many teen applications ask for it.
  • Availability matters! Tell them you can work after 3 PM on school days and weekends.
  • Don't just apply online — follow up with a call or visit the next day.
  • Dress neat when you drop off applications or go for an interview — first impressions count!
  • Chick-fil-A offers scholarships to Team Members — it's more than just a paycheck.
